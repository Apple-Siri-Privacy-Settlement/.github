# Apple Siri Privacy Settlement

Apple has agreed to pay **$95 million** as part of a class action settlement involving its Siri voice assistant. The lawsuit claimed that Siri **accidentally recorded users' private conversations** without consent, violating privacy rights. If you owned or used a Siri-enabled device between **September 17, 2014, and December 31, 2024**, you may be eligible to **receive up to $20** even without a claim code or proof of purchase.

---

## Who Is Eligible?

You may qualify if you:

- Owned or used any Siri-enabled Apple device from **Sept 17, 2014 to Dec 31, 2024**
- Experienced Siri activating unintentionally during a private conversation
- Submit a valid claim before the deadline

### Eligible Devices Include:

- iPhone  
- iPad  
- MacBook or iMac  
- Apple Watch  
- HomePod  
- iPod Touch  
- Apple TV (with Siri)

---

## How to File a Claim

Filing is easy and takes just a few minutes. Here’s how:

1. Go to the website 
 [lopez voice assistant](https://digiwebstory.com/apple-siri-privacy-settlement-claim/)

2. Click **“Submit Claim”**
3. Fill in your personal details (name, email, address)
4. Select the devices you owned or used
5. Confirm that Siri was accidentally triggered
6. Submit the form

 **No proof of purchase is required in many cases!**

---

##  Key Dates to Remember

- **Claim deadline:** July 2, 2025  
- **Final hearing:** August 1, 2025
